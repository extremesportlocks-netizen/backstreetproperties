What We Do
OUR SERVICES

Quality craftsmanship and attention to detail on every project. From small sidewalks to large retaining walls, we treat every job like it's our own property.

DRIVEWAYS

Custom concrete driveways engineered for Pittsburgh's tough winters. We pour thick, reinforced slabs with proper grading and drainage — built to handle freeze-thaw cycles for decades.

PATIOS

Transform your backyard with a custom concrete patio. Standard, stamped, or decorative finishes — we create outdoor living spaces that become the centerpiece of your home.

SIDEWALKS

Clean, level sidewalks that improve curb appeal and safety. We handle tear-outs, new pours, and ADA-compliant installations for residential and commercial properties.

DECORATIVE CONCRETE

Stamped patterns, exposed aggregate, colored concrete, and custom finishes. Get the look of natural stone or brick at a fraction of the cost — without sacrificing durability.

RETAINING WALLS

Structural retaining walls that hold back hillsides and transform sloped properties. Poured concrete, block, and segmental walls — all engineered and built to handle Pittsburgh's terrain.

CONCRETE REPAIR

Crack repair, resurfacing, leveling, and full tear-out replacements. If your existing concrete is failing, we'll assess the damage and give you an honest recommendation.

Our Story
MEET VINCE COLAIANNI

My name is Vince Colaianni, and I founded this company in 2021 with one goal in mind — to build things the right way.

Growing up, if I wasn't playing sports, I was right beside my grandfather. Whether we were pouring concrete, laying block, digging holes, or working in the garden, I was learning the trade hands-on. He taught me the old-school Italian way — take pride in your work, don't cut corners, and let your craftsmanship speak for itself.

"This business isn't just about concrete and construction — it's about legacy, discipline, and doing honest work that lasts."

Now I have three boys of my own, and they watch the grind the same way I once did. Just like my grandfather passed it down to me, I'm building something they can one day be proud of — not just a company, but a name that stands for quality and integrity.
